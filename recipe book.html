Szechwan Shrimp

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: Add some oomph to shrimp in this classic Szechwan-style seafood dish with a spicy red pepper, garlic and ginger sauce.
Ingredients Quantity
water 4 tablespoons
ketchup 2 tablespoons
soy sauce 1 tablespoon
cornstarch 2 teaspoons

Step 1: In a bowl, stir together water, ketchup, soy sauce, cornstarch, honey, crushed red pepper, and ground ginger. Set aside.

Step 2: Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Stir in green onions and garlic; cook 30 seconds. Stir in shrimp, and toss to coat with oil. Stir in sauce. Cook and stir until sauce is bubbly and thickened.
